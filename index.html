2. Training Programs

High-level view of how StarMove organizes training weeks and phases.

Phase 1
Foundation – 4 Day Split

Start with a 4-day structure: Chest/Triceps, Back/Biceps, Legs, Shoulders/Core. This phase lives inside the MuscleForge module.

Phase 2
Control & Technique

Focused on slower tempo, clean reps and controlled breathing. Ready to be documented in a dedicated StarMove /docs/ folder.

Phase 3
Performance & Flow

Integrates strength, conditioning and mental focus. Ideal place for combining MuscleForge, WarDance and SoulMotion later on.

3. Philosophy

StarMove treats training as a fusion of body, mind and spirit – not just workouts.

  • Body: Structure, strength, mobility and resilience.
  • Mind: Focus, discipline, clear progression and tracking.
  • Spirit: Intention, meaning and alignment with a higher path.

Every module in StarMove can include its own mindset section, daily cues and reflection prompts – all linked from this main hub.
